About Talk Green

As part of Qatar Sustainability Week 2021, Msheireb Properties is hosting the 1st edition of its annual sustainability event “Talk Green”, a 7-day event that enables organizations, institutions, and individuals to present and showcase their green programs, ideas, solutions, and initiatives to foster public awareness and participation. Each participant will have the opportunity to address a variety of eco-related solutions and strategies on sustainability.

Msheireb Downtown Doha is the beating heart of sustainability in Qatar, which is why the event is taking place there. The entire development is designed to consume fewer resources, generate less waste, lower costs, and achieve a reduced carbon footprint, making Msheireb Downtown Doha one of the world’s first sustainable regeneration project.

Join in on the green talks and exhibitions. The event is open for everyone.
